If one zero of the quadratic polynomial x² + 3x + k is 2, then the value of k is:
(A)10
(B)−10
(C)5
(D)−5
Given

Formula
Zero of p(x): if k is a zero of p(x), then p(k) = 0
Working

(B)A parabola opening downwards, touching the x-axis at exactly one point.
(C)A parabola opening upwards, completely above and not intersecting the x-axis.
