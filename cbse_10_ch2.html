01. Zero of a Linear Polynomial▼
Zero of ax + b
x = −ba = −(constant term)/(coefficient of x)
Σ2. Vieta's Formulae — Quadratic▼

For ax²+bx+c with zeroes α, β:

Sum of Zeroes
α+β = −ba
Product of Zeroes
αβ = ca
P3. Forming a Polynomial from Zeroes▼
From sum S, product P
k[x² − Sx + P]   (k ≠ 0)
²4. Key Identities for Zeroes▼
α²+β²
(α+β)² − 2αβ
(α−β)²

α⁻¹+β⁻¹
(α+β) / (αβ)
↗5. Geometrical Meaning of Zeroes▼
Key Rule
Zeroes of p(x) = x-coordinates where y = p(x) meets the x-axis
Quadratic
At most 2 zeroes (0, 1, or 2)
