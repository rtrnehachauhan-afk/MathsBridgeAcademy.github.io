2M
NCERT Ex 5.1PYQ 2020🟡 Medium
Given
Three terms: (a−b)², (a²+b²), (a+b)²
Formula

2a² + 2b² = 2a² + 2b² ✓

SA 18
A vacuum pump removes ¼ of air in a cylinder each time. Does the remaining air form an AP?
2M
NCERT Ex 5.1🟡 Medium
Given
Initial air = V. Each pump removes ¼ of remaining air.
Formula
d = a₂ − a₁
After pump 1: V − V/4 = 3V/4
After pump 2: 3V/4 − (1/4)(3V/4) = 3V/4 × 3/4 = 9V/16

d₁ = 9V/16 − 3V/4 = 9V/16 − 12V/16 = −3V/16
d₂ = 27V/64 − 9V/16 = 27V/64 − 36V/64 = −9V/64
d₁ ≠ d₂ → differences are not constant
∴ Not an AP — it forms a Geometric Progression.
AnswerNo — Not an AP

📋1. What is AP? How to identify?▾
General Form
a,  a+d,  a+2d,  a+3d,  …
a = first term  |  d = common difference = a₂−a₁ = a₃−a₂ = … (must always be same)
⚠ How to check if AP
Find a₂−a₁, a₃−a₂, a₄−a₃, … If ALL equal → AP. If even one differs → NOT AP.
🔢2. nth Term Formula▾
nth Term Formula
aₙ = a + (n−1)d
a = first term  |  d = common difference  |  n = position
Rearrangements
Find n:  n = (aₙ−a)/d + 1
Find d:  d = (aₙ−a)/(n−1)
kth from end:  l − (k−1)d
aₘ−aₙ shortcut:  (m−n)d
Why (n−1)?
1st term has 0 gaps before it. nth term has (n−1) gaps. Multiply d by (n−1).
∑3. Sum of First n Terms▾
Formula 1 — when a and d are known
Sₙ = n2[2a + (n−1)d]
Formula 2 — when first term a and last term l are known (FASTER)
Sₙ = n2(a + l)
Finding nth term from Sₙ
aₙ = Sₙ − Sₙ₋₁  (n≥2)   |   a₁ = S₁
💡4. Smart Substitutions▾
3 terms in AP
a−d,  a,  a+d
Sum=3a → find a first!
4 terms in AP
a−3d, a−d, a+d, a+3d
Sum=4a → find a first!
⚠ Common Mistakes
✗ Writing nd instead of (n−1)d
✗ Not checking n is a whole number
✗ Using Formula 1 when last term given (Formula 2 is faster)
✗ Forgetting a₁=S₁
